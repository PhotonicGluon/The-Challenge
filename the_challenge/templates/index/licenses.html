Songs

The songs used in The Challenge, which are the movements of Edvard Grieg's Peer Gynt Suite No. 1, Op. 46, are in the public domain.

  • The full recordings of the songs used in The Challenge can be found here on the Musopen website. Specifically, the four songs used are:
    • Peer Gynt Suite no. 1, Op. 46 - I. Morning Mood,
    • Peer Gynt Suite no. 1, Op. 46 - II. Aase's Death,
    • Peer Gynt Suite no. 1, Op. 46 - III. Anitra's Dance, and
    • Peer Gynt Suite no. 1, Op. 46 - IV. In the Hall Of The Mountain King,
    which were all performed by "Musopen Symphony".
  • As mentioned above, the recordings are in the public domain, as described here on the Creative Commons' website.
